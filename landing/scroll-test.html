📋 Manual Test Instructions

Due to browser security (CORS), the iframe controls may not work. To properly test:

  1. Open https://arkify.app in a new tab
  2. Scroll down to the very bottom of the page
  3. Verify you can see these 6 panels:
    • Top Left: COMMIT ANALYSIS (bar chart)
    • Top Right: REAL DATA METRICS (pie charts)
    • Middle Left: DECISION PATH (flowchart)
    • Middle Right: AGENT NETWORK (network diagram)
    • Bottom Left: ITERATIONS OVER TIME (line chart in yellow)
    • Bottom Right: META-RECURSION with ∞ symbol
  4. Also verify the footer is visible: "Your mistakes ARE your research | GitHub"

✅ Success criteria: All 6 panels fully visible + footer visible when scrolled to bottom

❌ Failure: Infinity symbol (∞) cut off or footer not visible even at max scroll
